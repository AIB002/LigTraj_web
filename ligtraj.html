Welcome to LigTraj

LigTraj is a comprehensive database focused on ligand-protein molecular dynamics trajectories. Browse the protein families in the sidebar to explore structure data and download trajectory packages for your research.

Key Features

  • Browse over 500 protein-ligand complexes across multiple protein families
  • View detailed binding information including affinity data and SMILES structures
  • Download ready-to-use molecular dynamics trajectory packages
  • Analyze trajectories with the LigTraj Python package

Select a protein family and PDB entry from the sidebar to begin exploring.
